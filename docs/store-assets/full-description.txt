Read any book in two languages at once. Mirrolit shows the original text and a live translation side by side, so you understand every line while you keep reading in the original.

Open an EPUB, FB2, or classic MOBI file and Mirrolit detects the source language automatically and translates into the language you choose — paragraph by paragraph, right next to the original.

WHY MIRROLIT

• Side-by-side reading — original on one side, translation on the other. No copy-pasting, no switching apps.
• Private by default — the built-in Google ML Kit engine translates fully on your device. Once a language pack is downloaded, it works offline and nothing you read is sent anywhere.
• Auto language detection — just open a book; Mirrolit figures out the source language for you.
• 12 target languages — English, Russian, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Portuguese, Arabic, Korean, and Turkish.

TRANSLATION ENGINES, YOUR CHOICE

• ML Kit (offline) — free, on-device, no setup.
• Quick Translate — fast online translation, no account needed.
• Bring your own key — DeepL, Google Cloud Translation, Microsoft Azure Translator, or LibreTranslate (including self-hosted) for advanced quality. API keys are stored encrypted on your device.

A LIBRARY THAT FEELS LIKE READING

• Beautiful, editorial typography — 4 themes (Paper, Sepia, Night, AMOLED) and 9 reading typefaces.
• Fine-tune everything — font size, line height, paragraph and letter spacing, indent, justification, and hyphenation.
• Inline illustrations in EPUB and FB2 books.
• Read aloud — built-in text-to-speech with adjustable rate and pitch.

FREE BOOKS BUILT IN

• Browse and download public-domain classics from Project Gutenberg and Standard Ebooks, right inside the app — no account, no cost.
• Import your own books from device storage or Google Drive.

TOOLS FOR LANGUAGE LEARNERS

• Tap and save words to your personal vocabulary.
• Add notes and bookmarks as you read.
• Track your progress in the Almanac — reading streak, minutes, pages, a 26-week activity heatmap, and time by book and language.
• Your reading position is remembered for every book.

PRIVACY FIRST

• No account required to read.
• Default translation runs on-device; your books and reading stats stay on your phone.
• Optional online engines only send text when you choose to enable them.
